## Functions

- `shield()` — Deposit tokens into privacy pool
- `unshield(amount, recipient)` — Withdraw from privacy pool
- `privateSwap(amountIn)` — Execute private swap
- `getShieldedBalance(user)` — Check shielded balance
- `getStats()` — Get total shielded, swaps, shield operations
